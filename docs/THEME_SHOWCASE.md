## 🎯 How to Use Themes

### Method 1: Theme Selector Page
1. Navigate to `/theme` in your app
2. Browse all 26 themes with live previews
3. Click on any theme card to apply it
4. See instant color changes!

### Method 2: Settings Page
1. Go to `/settings`
2. Look for theme options
3. Select your preferred theme

### Method 3: Navbar Toggle
- Click the sun/moon icon in the navbar
- Toggles between light and dark themes
